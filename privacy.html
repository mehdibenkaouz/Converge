This page explains how Converge processes data when you use the game (Android/TWA app and web version). If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use the app.

1) Controller and contact

Data controller: Mehdi Benkaouz
Privacy email: mehdibenkaouz00@gmail.com

2) Data that may be processed

A) Data provided by you (if you create an account)

  • Username and data required to sign in.
  • Credentials: if you use passwords, they are handled securely (not stored in plain text). If you use “passkeys”, we only keep the technical information needed for authentication (e.g., public keys).

B) Game data and progress

  • Progress, completed levels, hints/credits, settings (e.g., language, relax mode).
  • Some of this data may be saved locally on the device/browser (e.g., storage) and, if you log in, may also be saved server-side to sync across devices.

C) Technical data

  • IP, user-agent, date/time and technical logs (needed for security, debugging, abuse prevention and service operation).

D) Advertising (AdMob)

  • The app is supported by ads and may include rewarded ads.
  • Google AdMob may process advertising identifiers and device signals for ad delivery, frequency capping and measurement.
Useful link:
Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3) Purposes

  • Provide the game and features (login, saving progress, settings).
  • Security, abuse/fraud prevention, service stability.
  • Advertising to support the project (AdMob) and, if present, in‑game rewards linked to ads.
  • Support and responses to user requests (via email).

4) Sharing with third parties

We may share/allow processing of certain data with:

  • Google AdMob (advertising).
  • Technical providers used for hosting and delivery of the site/service (e.g., CDN/hosting) which may see technical logs like IP and requests, acting as processors.

We do not sell your personal data.

5) Retention and deletion

  • Account/progress data: kept as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.
  • Technical logs: kept for a limited time, as needed for security and diagnostics.

Account and data deletion:
You can request deletion:

  • In the app via the “Delete account” option.

    • Account deletion is performed inside the app via the “Delete account” button in Account settings.

6) Security

  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data (e.g., encrypted HTTPS transport, access controls, data minimization).
  • No system is perfect: if you find vulnerabilities, please report them via email.

7) User choices

  • You may not create an account (if available) and play without synchronization.
  • You can reset progress or delete local data (uninstall / clear browser storage).
  • Ad options depend on device settings and Google services.

8) Children

The app is not intentionally designed for children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child has provided data, contact us for removal.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this page. The “Last updated” date indicates the latest revision.

10) Data deletion without account removal

Users can reset their game progress inside the app via the “Reset progress” button. This removes saved progress data without deleting the account.
